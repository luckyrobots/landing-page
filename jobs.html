Work at Lucky Robots

We’re Lucky Robots. A small, sharp, fast-moving team building the next generation of robotics and AI. Our mission is to make robotics usable by regular software engineers—without ROS, without hardware, and without friction.

Instead of spending months debugging robot arms, we’re building powerful AI simulations using Lucky Engine, MuJoCo, and natural language interfaces—so robots can learn fast and perform better in the real world.
